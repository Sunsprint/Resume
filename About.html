Welcome.

Andrew   Chang

(702) 290-9043 | achang1@olin.edu

Hi! Introducing myself on a website seems so strange, but here we are. My name is Andrew! I love art and the act of making in whatever form it may assume (amongst other things.) You'll see showcases of my art and projects on this website. 

For some context, I'm from Las Vegas, NV, and attend Olin College of Engineering as a Mechanical Engineering major in the Class of 2024. I won the Congressional Art Award in 2019 for my painting Stone Dragon Eye ; the Congressional Committee took it upon themselves to both mispell my name and my piece title, though. I also won both structural and architectural awards at the International Bridge Competition in 2019 held at Johns Hopkins Univeristy.
