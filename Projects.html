2024 Jamaica Plain Train Park Soil Quality Study

As a part of Environmental Analysis and Science, I was part of a team which undertook field sampling and performed x-ray fluorescence and stereoscope analysis on the soil samples collected. The goal was to assess the contents of potentially-hazardous contaminants, such as lead or arsenic in a public park.

Report

2024 Water Quality Report

As a part of the Olin class Environmental Analysis and Science, I was a part of a team in charge of creating a report containing visualizations for and summarizing water quality data collection techniques and trends in the Charles River. This report was given to the Charles River Watershed Association, along with other class deliverables to advance the work in monitoring the water quality of the river.
